Wisphaven

Build, befriend, and protect your town of ghosts.

Wisphaven is an open-world, open-source, sandbox survival game. Summon ghastly residents to form your community, form relationships with them unleashing unique magical abilities, and construct automations to expand your reach.
Be wary of what you summon, as one wrong step may plunge your community into chaos!
Wisphaven is still in early development. Keep up with the journey on GitHub or on YouTube. Steam page coming soon!
